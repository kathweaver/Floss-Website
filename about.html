WELCOME to the website of Shade Tree Beagles. We are located in Southeast Iowa.

Shade Tree Kennels was established in 1990 when we acquired our first cocker spaniel—”Billger’s Presidential John Quincy”. In 2003 we added a beagle to the mix name K-Run Windkist Four on the Floor. In 2007 we had our first litter from Brach and Salli.

My first show dogs were cocker spaniels. Mick, a parti-color, still resides with us and is our last cocker spaniel. The Shade Tree kennel name was conceived by my mother. We have a very large tree that the kennel is built around. This large shade tree gives much needed shade and protection to the runs and kennels.

I am relatively new to the beagle world, but have fallen in love with this spirited, loving breed. I have wanted to be a part of this breed for a long time and am very happy to have made the transition from cocker spaniels to beagles. Both are great breeds to have in your life.

I would like to thank the following people for their guidance, friendship and patience in helping me in the dog world.
